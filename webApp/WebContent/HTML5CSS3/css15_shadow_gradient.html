21. What advice would you give to teams looking to improve their functional automated tests? Be flexible about the details, being defensive will stop you from innovating. Stay up-to-date with the latest tools and trends, but don’t jump on the hype train without doing an in-depth analysis. Be extra careful with assumptions, a browser is not just a JavaScript interpreter. If your website works on Chrome on Windows, it doesn’t automatically mean that it works on Chrome on MacOS or on Chrome on mobile devices. 22. What’s the one thing that every team should do, but most of them don’t? Testing on Safari, it’s the second most used browser. And it’s not going away anytime soon, it’s going to be even more popular. It keeps getting new features, Apple is determined to get even more of the market share from Chrome.
